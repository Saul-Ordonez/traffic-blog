This blog is all about how commuting is hard. And something needs to be done about it.

This is why it sucks:

  • It takes too long to get places
  • Gives people anxiety which shortens their life
  • Not eco friendly
  • People wait in traffic for an average of 15 years of their lives

What you can do to make it less shitty

  1. Learn how to merge
  2. Don't stop on the highway
  3. Take public transportation
  4. DON'T TEXT AND DRIVE
